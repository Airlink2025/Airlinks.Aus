A COMPREHENSIVE GUIDE TO DIFFERENT TYPES OF AIRCRAFT AND THEIR ROLES

Commercial Airliners

Purpose: Passenger & Cargo Transport

Large aircraft designed for carrying passengers and freight across various distances.

Boeing 737: Capacity: 126-230 passengers, Range: 3,300-4,400 miles, Speed: 530 mph

Airbus A320: Capacity: 140-180 passengers, Range: 3,300-4,000 miles, Speed: 515 mph

Boeing 777: Capacity: 314-396 passengers, Range: 5,240-9,700 miles, Speed: 560 mph

Airbus A380: Capacity: 525-853 passengers, Range: 8,000 miles, Speed: 560 mph

Fighter Jets

Purpose: Military Combat & Defense

High-speed military aircraft designed for air-to-air combat and ground attack missions.

F-16 Fighting Falcon: Speed: Mach 2+, Range: 2,622 miles, Weapons: Various missiles and guns

F-15 Eagle: Speed: Mach 2.5, Range: 3,450 miles, Role: Air superiority fighter

F-22 Raptor: Speed: Mach 2.25, Range: 1,600 miles, Features: Stealth technology

F-35 Lightning II: Speed: Mach 1.6, Range: 1,200 miles, Role: Multi-role stealth fighter

Cargo Aircraft

Purpose: Freight Transportation

Specialized aircraft designed to transport goods, equipment, and materials worldwide.

Boeing 747-8F: Payload: 140 tons, Range: 4,390 miles, Cargo Volume: 858 cubic meters

Airbus A330-200F: Payload: 70 tons, Range: 4,000 miles, Cargo Volume: 475 cubic meters

Antonov An-124: Payload: 150 tons, Range: 2,500 miles, Role: Heavy cargo transport

C-130 Hercules: Payload: 20 tons, Range: 2,360 miles, Role: Military cargo transport

Private Jets

Purpose: Business & Personal Travel

Smaller aircraft designed for private transportation with luxury amenities and flexibility.

Gulfstream G650: Passengers: 8-18, Range: 7,000 miles, Speed: 610 mph

Bombardier Global 7500: Passengers: 8-17, Range: 7,700 miles, Speed: 590 mph

Cessna Citation X: Passengers: 8-12, Range: 3,460 miles, Speed: 717 mph

Embraer Phenom 300: Passengers: 6-11, Range: 2,010 miles, Speed: 464 mph

Helicopters

Purpose: Vertical Flight Operations

Rotorcraft capable of vertical takeoff, landing, and hovering in place for specialized missions.

Bell 407: Passengers: 6, Range: 374 miles, Speed: 140 mph, Role: Utility helicopter

Sikorsky UH-60 Black Hawk: Passengers: 11, Range: 368 miles, Speed: 183 mph

AH-64 Apache: Crew: 2, Range: 300 miles, Speed: 182 mph, Role: Attack helicopter

CH-47 Chinook: Payload: 12.7 tons, Range: 400 miles, Speed: 196 mph

Training Aircraft

Purpose: Pilot Education & Training

Aircraft specifically designed for teaching new pilots with dual controls and safety features.

Cessna 172: Passengers: 4, Speed: 140 mph, Range: 640 miles, Role: Primary trainer

Piper Cherokee: Passengers: 4, Speed: 140 mph, Range: 640 miles, Role: Flight training

T-6 Texan II: Crew: 2, Speed: 362 mph, Range: 1,036 miles, Role: Military trainer

Diamond DA40: Passengers: 4, Speed: 147 mph, Range: 750 miles, Role: Modern trainer

Supersonic Aircraft

Purpose: High-Speed Transportation

Aircraft capable of flying faster than the speed of sound for rapid long-distance travel.

Concorde: Passengers: 92-128, Speed: Mach 2.04, Range: 4,500 miles (Retired 2003)

SR-71 Blackbird: Crew: 2, Speed: Mach 3.3, Range: 2,900 miles, Role: Reconnaissance

Boom Overture: Passengers: 65-88, Speed: Mach 1.7, Range: 4,250 miles (In development)

Seaplanes

Purpose: Water-Based Operations

Aircraft designed to take off and land on water surfaces for coastal and island transportation.

De Havilland DHC-2 Beaver: Passengers: 6, Speed: 158 mph, Range: 778 miles

Cessna 208 Caravan: Passengers: 9-14, Speed: 214 mph, Range: 1,070 miles

Icon A5: Passengers: 2, Speed: 109 mph, Range: 427 miles, Role: Sport flying
